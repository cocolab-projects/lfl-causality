Scenario: In this HIT, imagine that you are an employee of "ChemCo", a company which studies chemicals on other planets. Your goal in this HIT is to learn how to identify the causes of chemical reactions.

Explorer: You are the explorer, and you will be able to interact with chemicals to learn about what type of reactions occur. You will be able to experiment with the chemicals as much as you want. When you are ready to proceed, you will continue to a chatroom where you will communicate your findings about the chemicals. These findings will be used to test a later student (your partner), and you will recieve a bonus based on that student's performance. This later student will only have access to your findings, and won't be able to experiment, so make sure to be thorough. You will then be given a series of around 10 questions about the chemicals. Once you have finished with one set of chemicals, you will continue, repeating this process (exploration, chatroom communication, assessment) for a total of 3 different groups of chemicals.

Bonus: At the end of each test, you will receive a score: (# of questions correctly answered) At the very end, we will sum together your scores across the rounds with your partner's scores across the rounds. This will be the total score for both players. You will each get a bonus of $0.01 per point, so pay attention and communicate effectively!
